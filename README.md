#  Good Luck Coffee – Sales & Expansion Analysis (SQL)

##  Project Overview
Good Luck Coffee is an online coffee brand that has been selling products across India since January 2023.  
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance and consumer demand to identify the top three Indian cities with the highest potential for opening new physical coffee shop locations.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends  
- Evaluate city-level demand to assess market potential  
- Study monthly sales growth patterns  
- Recommend the top three cities for store expansion based on data-driven insights  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Database: RDBMS  
- Language: SQL  
- Concepts & Techniques Used:
  - Multiple table JOINS  
  - Common Table Expressions (CTEs)  
  - Window Functions  
  - Subqueries  
  - Aggregations and filtering  

---

##  Key Business Questions Solved
1. Total Revenue from Coffee Sales  
   - Calculated overall and city-level revenue to understand sales contribution.
     
2. Market Potential Analysis  
   - Analyzed customer demand and sales distribution across cities to identify high-potential markets.

3. Monthly Sales Growth Analysis  
   - Evaluated month-over-month sales trends to understand growth patterns and consistency.

---

##  Key Insights & Recommendations 

City 1: Pune
	1.Average rent per customer is very low which is 294
	2.Highest total revenue.
	3.Average sales per customer is also high above 24k

City 2: Delhi
	1.Highest estimated coffee consumers at 7.7 million.
	2.Highest total number of customers, which is 68.
	3.Average rent per customer is 330 (still under 500).

City 3: Jaipur
	1.Highest number of customers, which is 69.
	2.Average rent per customer is very low at 156.
	3.Average sales per customer is better at 11.6k.

---

##  Outcome
This analysis provided data-backed recommendations to support Good Luck Coffee’s physical store expansion strategy by identifying the most promising cities based on consumer demand and sales performance.


Identified cities with consistently high sales and customer demand.

Analyzed revenue contribution to determine market strength by city.

Observed monthly growth trends, highlighting stable and high-growth periods.

Based on demand and performance metrics, top three cities were recommended for opening new coffee shop locations.
